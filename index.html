About Me

I am Haoran Yu, a researcher and statistics professional pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Mathematics at the University of Toronto, with minors in Statistics and Economics. My academic background in probability, statistics, financial economics, data analysis, and machine learning has provided me with a strong foundation in quantitative methods. I leverage these skills to develop robust analytical frameworks and predictive models for complex real-world challenges, while continuously expanding my expertise.

I am proficient in Python (including libraries such as NumPy, pandas, Matplotlib, PyTorch, SciPy, and scikit-learn), R, SAS, and SQL.
